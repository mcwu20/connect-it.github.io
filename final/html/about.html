AptPan is a cooking pan that tracks your cooking process through an advanced sensor technology. The sensor automatically detects the type of food that is in the pan. Based on the weight and size of the food, it will create a suitable cooking time. The sensor will continuously provide data on the cooking process so users can make food like medium-rare beef or soft yolk eggs just the way they want it!
